About

I am currently working on practicing and improving my programming abilities in order to start a career in a fun and exciting industry. I got into programming after taking an intro to programming class in high school and discovering that I very much enjoyed the challenge and feeling of accomplishment that comes with it. Afterwards, I went to a technical college in order to further my education.

Currently, I am knowledgeable in both C++ and C#, and I am also working on broadening my field of knowledge by learning the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I have not at this time had any experience in a professional setting, but I do have experience working in Unity and GameMaker Studio to practice making small games and applications.

I am confident I can succeed in this industry. At the moment, I simply need to get that hands on experience and benefit from working with a team of skilled individuals who can help me get the knowledge and know-how to be a valuable asset to any team. As an aside, I am also interested in perhaps pursuing a career in an editorial position as I enjoy reading, writing, and helping others.
